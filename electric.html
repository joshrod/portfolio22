Overview

Montoya Electric, a local electrical contractor working out of Chicago, realized the importance of creating an online brand to further legitimize their business and improve sales potential.

Looking at the demographic of the client's customers, I realized that cross-browser compatibility was paramount, and that information needed to be easily accessible upon first arriving to the site.

The design I chose to go with for this website focused on the company's services and electrical experience, while presenting a modern aesthetic that the user will enjoy looking at.

Development of this project was speedy and efficient. Going the route of a static website ensured that loading times would be fast and no unnecessary dependencies would be needed.
