Overview

Freetail Brewing Company, a popular San Antonio brewery, required an updated web experience reflective of their gritty branding and approach to crafting innovative beer flavors.

The team decided that it would be best to highlight the most important aspects of the brewing company in the site's navigation, while making the core beers the "pièce de résistance" of the site.

The design of the website focuses on giving the user facts at a glance along with engaging, modern visuals and layouts to keep them interested.

The core beers, however, provide a dynamic, scrollable experience that highlights Freetail's tried-and-true brews.
